Bagasbas Beach

Bagasbas Beach in Daet, Camarines Norte is among the earliest recorded Philippine surfing destinations and its consistent strong waves throughout the year make it an ideal surfing spot for beginners and pros. The mighty waves come from the Pacific Ocean and land at Bagasbas' powdery gray and dark brown sands.

Location

Bagasbas Beach is located in Barangay Bagasbas, Daet, Camarines Norte

Longest boulevard in the Philippines. 8.7 kilometers long and will pass through the towns of Mercedes, Daet, Talisay and Vinzons. Bagasbas Beach Boulevard.
