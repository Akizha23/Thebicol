Mayong-payong

Mayong Payong, also known as Mount Mayong Payong or Umbrella Hills, is a scenic mountain peak located in Mobo, Masbate, Philippines. This emerging tourist destination offers panoramic views of Masbate City, Ticao Island, and the surrounding landscapes, making it a popular spot for nature enthusiasts and photographers.

Location


Mayong Payong is located in Barangay Usab, Mobo, Masbate, Philippines. It is a well-known highland destination offering breathtaking panoramic views of Masbate City, the surrounding islands, and the lush landscapes of the province.
